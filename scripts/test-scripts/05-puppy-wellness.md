# Test Script 5: Puppy Wellness Visit

**Scenario:** Routine puppy vaccination and health check

---

*[Start recording]*

"This is a puppy wellness visit. We have Luna, a 12 week old female Golden Retriever puppy. She currently weighs 8 kilograms.

The breeder provided first vaccination at 8 weeks, so she's here today for her second set of puppy shots. Owner reports she's been doing great at home, eating well, very playful and energetic.

She's been having normal bowel movements, no diarrhea or vomiting. She's on a large breed puppy food, three meals a day. She's drinking water normally.

Owner has started basic house training, going okay so far with a few accidents. She's sleeping through the night now.

Let's do her exam. Vitals first. Temperature is 38.9, which is normal for an excited puppy. Heart rate is 140, respiratory rate 30.

General appearance, she's bright, alert, and very wiggly. Good body condition, I'd say 5 out of 9, nice and lean.

Eyes are clear, no discharge. Ears are clean, no odor or discharge. Looking in her mouth, she has her deciduous teeth, nice pink gums, no abnormalities.

Auscultating the heart... I'm hearing a soft grade 2 out of 6 systolic murmur, left apex. This is likely a puppy murmur, which is common and usually resolves. We should recheck this at her next visit.

Lungs are clear. Abdomen is soft, no pain, no organomegaly. Umbilicus is normal. Palpating the femoral pulses, they're strong and synchronous.

Lymph nodes all normal size. Skin and coat look healthy, no parasites visible.

We'll give her DHPP vaccine today and schedule her final puppy vaccine for 16 weeks. We should also discuss spay timing and heartworm prevention."

*[Stop recording]*

---

**Expected differentials:** Healthy puppy, incidental innocent heart murmur
**Expected triage:** Routine/Wellness
