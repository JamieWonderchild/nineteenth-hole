# England Golf WHS Integration — ISV Licence Proposal

**Prepared for:** Club Captain
**Date:** April 2026
**Subject:** Integrating official World Handicap System data into our club management platform

---

## What We Are Proposing

We are applying to become a licensed **Independent Software Vendor (ISV)** under England Golf's World Handicap System (WHS) programme. This would allow our club management software — built specifically for our club — to connect directly to the national handicap database and handle handicap administration digitally, end-to-end.

---

## Why This Matters

Right now, our members manage handicaps separately from everything else we do in the club — competitions, tee times, pools, and games all live in our app, but handicaps still rely on a third-party platform. This creates friction:

- Members use multiple apps and logins
- Competition results have to be submitted manually to the WHS system
- Handicap indices are not visible alongside competition entries or round history

With this integration, everything would live in one place.

---

## What the ISV Licence Gives Us

England Golf contracts a company called **DotGolf** to operate the WHS platform for England, Wales, and Ireland. Once licensed, we gain full access to their secure API, which allows our software to:

| Capability | What This Means |
|---|---|
| **Read Handicap Indices** | Display each member's current Handicap Index and Low Handicap Index inside our app |
| **Post Competition Scores** | Automatically submit qualifying competition scores to the CDH after finalisation |
| **Access Scoring Records** | View a member's last 20 differentials, best 8 scores highlighted |
| **Verify Visitor Handicaps** | Look up any visiting golfer's CDH ID and Handicap Index in real time |
| **Receive Updates via Webhook** | Get notified when handicap indices change overnight — no manual refresh needed |
| **Playing Conditions Calculation** | Pull PCC data for any given competition date |

In short: anything Intelligent Golf or Club V1 can do with handicaps, we can do too.

---

## Who Else Is Licensed

Every major UK golf software platform holds this licence. Current England Golf accredited ISVs include:

- **Intelligent Golf** (used by 800+ clubs including Wentworth and Royal Birkdale)
- **Club Systems International (Club V1)**
- **HandicapMaster**
- **Golf Genius**
- **GolfBox**

We would be joining the same tier as these established platforms.

---

## Cost

The licence fee is set by England Golf at:

> **£74.50 + VAT per club, per six months** (~£149 + VAT per year)

This is a flat fee per club, regardless of membership size. It covers the right to connect to the WHS API and is payable to England Golf annually.

There are no per-transaction or per-member charges beyond this.

## What We Are Asking the Club For

1. **Endorsement** — A letter or email from the club confirming we are the club's software provider and that the integration serves the club's members. England Golf will expect to understand the context of the application.

2. **Annual fee contribution** — £149 + VAT/year. This can be absorbed into the platform subscription or billed separately — your preference.

3. **Handicap Secretary involvement** — Brief input from our Handicap Secretary during the accreditation phase to confirm our score submission process is correct.
